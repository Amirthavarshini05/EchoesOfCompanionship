Project Overview

Project Summary

The Problem

Social isolation among elderly individuals in India represents a critical yet often hidden health crisis affecting millions. Traditional monitoring systems focus only on physical emergencies like falls, missing the subtle behavioral changes that indicate social withdrawal. This reactive approach fails to address the gradual decline in social engagement that can lead to depression, cognitive decline, and other serious health complications.

Current solutions are either too intrusive (cameras, voice recorders) or too limited (fall detection only), creating a significant gap in proactive mental health monitoring for the elderly population.

Our Solution

Echoes of Companionship is a privacy-first AI system that proactively detects social isolation through non-intrusive ambient sensors. Using advanced AI techniques including Multi-modal Fusion, Time-Series Anomaly Detection, and Causal Inference, the system learns each individual's unique behavioral patterns and identifies subtle deviations that signal social withdrawal.

The system employs a privacy-first design using non-recording microphones, motion sensors, and smart device logs that never capture personal conversations or images. All raw data is processed locally on ESP32 microcontrollers within the home, ensuring sensitive information never leaves the premises.

Our personalized baseline approach allows the AI to learn each individual's unique behavioral patterns, avoiding false alarms that would occur with generic standards. The system provides proactive alerts by detecting social withdrawal trends early, before they become severe health issues, and delivers actionable insights to caregivers with specific recommendations for gentle interventions.

The system provides caregivers with timely, actionable insights, enabling early intervention through gentle nudges before social isolation becomes severe. By focusing on psychological well-being rather than just physical safety, we offer a more compassionate and effective approach to elderly care that respects privacy while ensuring no one suffers in silence.
